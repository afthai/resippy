Tasty liquids to put on other stuff



Cooking oil

▼

Original recipe: My mom

Ingredients

  • However many green onions your heart desires (usually like 1 stick for 1 meal tho lol)
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce (preferably low sodium, but normal is fine as long as you don't let it boil for too long)

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice up the green onions.
  2. Cook the green onions in the olive oil in a pan over medium high heat.
  3. When a few of the onions start to turn brown and crispy on the edges, add in as much soy sauce as there is oil.
  4. When the soy sauce goes all bubbly bubbly (which may be right away if your heat is high), take it off of the stove.
  5. If the sauce is too salty you can add a little bit of sugar to mellow it out. Otherwise, pour over vegetables/wontons/rice/soup/etc for a bit of extra fun!
