Garlic and cheese biscuits

▼

Original recipe: Sugar Spun Run

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp salted butter
  • 1 cup flour, sifted
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar, tightly packed
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried garlic
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or half a lemon squeezed into a half cup container, and fill the rest of the way with milk)/li>
  • 1 cup sharp chedder cheese, thick shred

Topping

  • 2 tbsp salted butter
  • 1 tsp dried garlic
  • additional salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter and then let it cool to room temp.
  2. Combine all the dry ingredients, except for cheese.
  3. Combine the butter and buttermilk in a separate bowl.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Gently mix in the cheese
  6. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Farenheit.
  7. Spoon the batter onto a baking sheet in about 3 tbsp sized blobs. Space them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  9. Meanwhile, melt the butter for the topping and mix in the garlic and any additional salt.
  10. When the biscuits are done cooking, spoon the topping over each one.
  11. Nom nom tasty biscuits
