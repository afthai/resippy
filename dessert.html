Brown sugar cookies

▼

Original recipe: Sugar Spun Run

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 3/4 cup dark brown sugar, tightly packed
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups flour, sifted
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Combine all the wet ingredients in one bowl, and all the dry ingrediants in another bowl.
  3. Mix all of them together.
  4. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in refridgerator for an hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  6. Roll cookie dough into balls about the size of golf balls. Place them on a pan about 2 inches apart. Lightly press on the tops to flatten them slightly into fat hockey pucks.
  7. Bake for 12 minutes. Don't worry if the cookies don't look done in the center, they will cool to doneness.

Tips

  • Let cookies cool on the sheet after cooking. They will fall apart if you try to move them while they are hot.


Chocolate buttercream frosting

▼

Original recipe: Me! :D

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups unsalted butter
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp coffee

Instructions

  1. Beat the butter until fluffy. Do not let it melt though.
  2. Mix in the powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and coffee. Add milk as necessary, stop at preferred texture. Beat until well combined.
  3. Use to frost cake, cupcakes, cookies, or just eat it out of the bowl!

Tips

  • If it becomes too liquid while mixing, putting it in the fridge for half an hour should firm it back up.


Sweet mung bean soup

▼

Original recipe: My mother

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 1.5 cups mung beans
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (dark or light is fine, I prefer dark)

Instructions

  1. Don't need to wash or soak the beans. Just put them in a pot with the water and sugar.
  2. Boil for at least half an hour. You can boil longer if you want it to be thicker/goopier.
  3. Yayyy now you can eat it! I like to eat it chilled, but warm is also good.
