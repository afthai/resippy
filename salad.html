Bastardized caprese salad

▼

Original recipe: My mom

Ingredients

  • An amount of cherry tomatoes, the more colorful the better
  • Fresh mozzerella, 2/3 as much as the cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil, a handful is usually good enough, but add more or less depending on your preference
  • Little bit of olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic (the more the merrier imo)

Instructions

  1. Dice the cheese, mince the garlic, chop the basil into a normal godly size.
  2. Combine everything in a large bowl and toss until everything is well mixed and evenly coated with the oil/salt/garlic.
  3. Eat it and question why you didn't make a normal caprese salad but dismiss the thought because this is good enough if you aren't around any Italians.
